About Me


Hi, I am Kritika Naagar (AKA Leaf). I am a futurist, a programmer, a student, a developer, a space enthusiast and in general a techie. I am currently in high school and love doing things beyond academics. I was introduced to programming by Khan Academy that I previously used for Math and Science. I started my journey with p5.js (Processing JS) and then learned HTML, CSS and SQL. Later I jumped to freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, w3schools, Scrimba and Youtube where I learned ES6 Javascript, jQuery, SASS, Vue.js, Python, Node.js and so much more. I am still learning various technologies, participating in hackathons and competitions and international/national level tests.
